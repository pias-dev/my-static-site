How to resize an image online

  1. Drag & drop an image or click “Select an Image”.
  2. Enter width/height or pick a preset. Toggle “Lock Aspect Ratio” if needed.
  3. Click “Apply Resize” to preview.
  4. Choose PNG/JPG/WEBP and click “Download Image”.
