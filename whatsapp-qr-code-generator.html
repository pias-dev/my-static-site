How to create a WhatsApp QR code

  1. Enter your full WhatsApp number in international format (no + or spaces).
  2. Optional: add a prefilled message.
  3. Customize colors and optionally upload a logo.
  4. Click “Generate” to preview, then download PNG or PDF.
  5. Test by scanning with your phone (Android or iOS camera/WhatsApp app).

Tip: Keep strong contrast (dark foreground on light background) for best scan results.

Best practices

  • Use high contrast (dark foreground, light background) and avoid busy backgrounds.
  • If you use a logo, keep it modest (10–25% of QR width) to preserve scanability.
  • Use the full international number without the + and double check digits.
  • Test on multiple devices and apps; keep a plain version (no logo) as a fallback.
  • Print at sufficient size (at least 2.5–3 cm on paper) for reliable scans.
