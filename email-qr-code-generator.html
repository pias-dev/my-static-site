Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your data is never uploaded to our servers.

Which email apps are supported?

Works with Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, Yahoo Mail, and most default mail apps on iOS and Android.

Can I include line breaks in the message?

Yes. We URL encode the content, and most mail apps handle line breaks correctly in the body.

Any limits on subject/body length?

Some scanners/mail apps have URL length limits. For best compatibility, keep the total under ~2,000 characters.

Can I use a logo?

Yes. We use high error correction (H) so logos in the center scan reliably. Keep good color contrast.

Is it free forever?

Yes lifetime free and unlimited. No signup, no paywalls, no watermarks.
