How to scan a QR code from an image

  1. Drag & drop a photo/screenshot, or click “Select an Image.”
  2. We process it locally in your browser and show the result.
  3. Copy the decoded text or open the link. For Wi Fi, copy SSID/password.

Works on desktop and mobile. No data ever leaves your device.

Best practices for clean scans

  • Use clear, high contrast images (dark QR on light background).
  • Cropped/blurred images reduce accuracy include some margin.
  • Avoid heavy logos over the center finder patterns.
  • Try another screenshot if the QR is tiny or low resolution.
