Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to All Tool AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personally identifiable information (PII) to use our tools. Our data collection is limited to the following:

  • Uploaded Files: For tools that require file uploads (e.g., Image Tools, PDF Tools), your files are temporarily transferred to our servers for processing.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal, anonymous information about your interaction with our site, such as the tools you use and the frequency of your visits, to help us improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services:

  • Client-Side Processing: Whenever possible, our tools process your data directly within your browser. In these cases, your information is never sent to our servers.
  • Server-Side Processing: For more complex tasks, your data is processed on our servers. We use this data only to perform the requested operation.
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us understand which tools are most popular and how we can enhance the user experience.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We respect your privacy and do not store your data longer than necessary. Our data retention policy is simple:

  • Files uploaded to our servers are automatically and permanently deleted within a few hours of processing.
  • We do not keep any backups of your files. Once deleted, they are gone forever.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transfers between your browser and our servers are encrypted using SSL/TLS.
  • Our servers are configured with security best practices to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies, primarily for functionality such as remembering your preferred theme (light or dark mode). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third-party services, except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., hosting providers). We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
