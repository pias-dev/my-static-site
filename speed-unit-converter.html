Formulas and quick reference

  • mph to m/s: m/s = mph × 0.44704
  • km/h to m/s: m/s = km/h ÷ 3.6
  • ft/s to m/s: m/s = ft/s × 0.3048
  • knots to m/s: m/s = kn × 0.514444

1 m/s equals:

  • 3.6 km/h
  • 2.23694 mph
  • 3.28084 ft/s
  • 1.94384 kn

Best practices

  • Check local units (mph vs km/h) when sharing speed values across regions.
  • Round only at the end if you’ll do further calculations.
  • Keep original values for auditing and records.
