How to rotate or delete PDF pages

  1. Upload your PDF (drag & drop or select file).
  2. Use the rotate 🔄 button on each page (90° per click) or “Rotate All.”
  3. Use the delete ❌ button to remove unwanted pages.
  4. Optionally “Reset All” to undo changes.
  5. Click “Save & Download PDF” to export your edited file.

Tip: For very large PDFs, edit in parts for smoother performance.

Best practices

  • Review thumbnail previews after rotating or deleting pages.
  • Remove passwords before editing; re-export protected PDFs first.
  • Keep original files as a backup before saving changes.
  • Test the downloaded PDF to confirm orientation and page order.
