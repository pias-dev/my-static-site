Best practices

  • Know if your value is gauge (psig) or absolute (psia) before converting or sharing.
  • Use standard conditions for column units (mmHg, inHg, mmH₂O, inH₂O) temperature affects density.
  • Round only at the end if you’ll do further calculations.
  • Keep original values and unit notes for audits and specs.
