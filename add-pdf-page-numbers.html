How to add page numbers to a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF (drag & drop or click Select a PDF).
  2. Choose your format using {page} and {total} (e.g., “Page {page} of {total}”).
  3. Set page range, font, size, color, and alignment.
  4. Check the live preview, then click “Apply & Download”.
  5. Your numbered PDF is saved instantly no uploads, no watermarks.

Tip: To skip numbering a cover page, set the range starting at page 2 or higher.

Best practices

  • Use clear, consistent text (e.g., “{page}” or “Page {page} of {total}”).
  • Choose readable sizes (10–14 pt) and strong contrast.
  • Center alignment is most readable; left/right works for branded layouts.
  • Use page ranges to exclude covers, blank pages, or TOCs as needed.
  • Always review the live preview before downloading.
