How to create a vCard QR code

  1. Enter your contact details: name, email, phone, company, title, and address.
  2. Optional: add website and social links.
  3. Customize colors and optionally upload a logo.
  4. Click “Generate” to preview, then download PNG or PDF.
  5. Test by scanning with a phone camera or QR app on iOS/Android.

Tip: Use strong contrast (dark foreground on light background) for the best scan results.

Best practices

  • Prefer black/dark foreground on white/light background.
  • Keep logos modest (10–25% width) and leave quiet zone margins.
  • Double-check names, numbers, and email spelling before sharing.
  • Test with multiple phones and apps; keep a version without logo as backup.
  • Print at 300+ DPI for small items like business cards.
