Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EMI and how is it calculated?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) = P × r × (1+r)n / [(1+r)n − 1], where P is principal, r is monthly rate (annual/12), and n is months.

Does it include taxes/insurance/fees?

No results show principal and interest only. Add taxes, insurance, PMI/HOA, or fees separately if needed.

Can I use it worldwide?

Yes. It supports fixed rate loans globally and aligns with terms like EMI (India), APR (US), and home loan (UK/AU).

Is my data private and secure?

Yes everything is calculated in your browser. Your information never leaves your device.

Interest rate vs APR?

The interest rate covers borrowing the principal. APR includes certain fees and helps compare loan offers.

What about extra payments?

This version models fixed payments. To model extra payments, reduce the principal and recalc, or contact us to add an extra payment feature.
