Frequently Asked Questions

Radians vs degrees what’s the difference?

Degrees split a circle into 360 parts; radians use arc length relative to radius (2π rad in a full turn).

How do I convert degrees to radians?

radians = degrees × π/180. Example: 90° = π/2 rad.

What is a turn (revolution)?

One full circle. 1 rev = 360° = 2π rad = 400 gon.

What are arcminutes and arcseconds?

1° = 60′ (arcminutes); 1′ = 60″ (arcseconds). Useful for fine angular measurements.

What about MOA vs mrad for shooting?

1 MOA = 1/60°. 1 mrad = 0.001 rad. At 100 yd, 1 MOA ≈ 1.047 in; 0.1 mrad ≈ 0.36 in. Check scope specs.
