How to calculate compound interest

  1. Enter your principal amount and annual rate (APR).
  2. Select compounding frequency: daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annually.
  3. Choose duration by Years/Months/Days or use exact Start/End dates.
  4. Click “Calculate” to see future value, total interest, and detailed breakdowns.

Formula: A = P × (1 + r/n)n×t, where P = principal, r = annual rate (decimal), n = compounds per year, t = years.

Is it private and secure?

What compounding frequencies can I use?

Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annually. You can also calculate using exact dates.

What is APY vs APR?

APR is annual rate without compounding. APY includes compounding, which makes returns higher for the same APR with more frequent compounding.
