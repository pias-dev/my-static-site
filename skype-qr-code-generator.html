How to create a Skype QR code

  1. Enter your Skype ID or the email linked to your Skype account.
  2. Choose the action: Add Contact, Start Chat, Start Voice Call, or Start Video Call.
  3. Optional: customize colors and upload a logo.
  4. Click “Generate” to preview, then download PNG or PDF.
  5. Test by scanning with a phone camera or QR app. Ensure Skype opens as expected.

Tip: Keep strong contrast (dark foreground on light background) and allow clear margins for best scan results.

Best practices

  • High contrast (dark on light) and a quiet zone around the QR are essential.
  • If you use a logo, keep it modest (10–25% of QR width) to preserve scanability.
  • Test across devices and apps (iOS, Android, Windows, macOS).
  • Use a short, accurate Skype ID or the correct email for reliable resolution.
  • Keep a plain version (no logo) as a fallback for tiny prints.
