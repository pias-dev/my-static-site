How to calculate percentages (formulas)

  1. What is X% of Y: result = (X/100) × Y
  2. X is what percent of Y: percent = (X ÷ Y) × 100
  3. Percentage change: change% = ((Final − Initial) ÷ |Initial|) × 100
  4. Value after change: new = Initial × (1 ± percent/100)
  5. Reverse percentage (find original): original = Final ÷ (1 ± percent/100)
