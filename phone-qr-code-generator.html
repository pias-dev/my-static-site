How to create a phone number QR code

  1. Enter your phone number in international format (e.g., +1 555 123 4567).
  2. Optional: add Contact Name to generate a vCard (saves contact on scan).
  3. Customize colors and optionally upload a logo.
  4. Click “Generate” to preview, then download PNG or PDF.
  5. Test by scanning with an iPhone/Android camera or QR app.

Tip: Use a dark foreground on a light background and keep the logo ≤ 25% of the QR width for best results.

Best practices

  • Format: Use E.164 (include country code), e.g., +44..., +61..., +91....
  • Contrast: Dark foreground on light background; avoid busy or inverted backgrounds.
  • Logo: Keep modest (10–25% of QR width); ensure there’s quiet zone (margin) around the code.
  • Size: For print, use at least 2–3 cm (0.8–1.2 in) with clear margins; larger for scans at distance.
  • Testing: Check across multiple devices and apps; keep a plain (no logo) fallback if needed.
