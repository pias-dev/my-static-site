How is Chronological Age Calculated?

This chronological age calculator measures the exact period from your birth date up to today’s date. It subtracts your birth year, month, and day from the present time of year, month, and day making proper adjustments for leap years to give you an answer in years, months, and days.

Why do I need an “how old am I” calculator? It's by far the quickest and most precise way of finding an answer! Be it filling out an official form, planning for a milestone birthday, or out of sheer curiosity, the tool provides, at once and math-free, an extremely detailed breakdown on age.
