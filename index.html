Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do you calculate chronological age?

Our chronological age calculator determines the precise time elapsed from your date of birth to the current date. It subtracts your birth year, month, and day from today's date, adjusting for leap years to give you a result in years, months, and days.

Why should I use this "how old am I" calculator?

It's the fastest and easiest way to get an exact answer! Whether you're filling out an official form, planning for a milestone birthday, or just curious, our tool gives you a detailed age breakdown instantly without any math.

Is my date of birth information saved?

Absolutely not. Your privacy is our number one concern. The entire calculation happens securely in your web browser. Your DOB is never sent to our servers, ensuring your data remains 100% private and confidential worldwide.
