How to create a URL QR code

  1. Paste your URL (e.g., https://example.com).
  2. Customize foreground/background colors and optionally upload a logo.
  3. Click “Generate” to preview the QR code.
  4. Download as PNG or PDF and test by scanning on multiple devices.

Tip: Use strong contrast (dark foreground on light background) and keep a clear quiet zone around the code.

Best practices

  • High contrast improves scan reliability across cameras.
  • Keep logos modest (10–25% of QR width) and avoid busy backgrounds.
  • Use sufficiently large QR sizes for print; test from typical viewing distance.
  • Always scan test before publishing; keep a plain (no logo) fallback.
  • Use SVG/PNG in web and high DPI PDF for print.
