How to rotate or flip an image online

  1. Drag & drop an image or click “Select an Image”.
  2. Use Rotate Left/Right for 90° steps, and the Straighten slider for fine control.
  3. Flip horizontally or vertically to mirror your photo.
  4. Choose PNG/JPG/WEBP and click “Download Image”.
