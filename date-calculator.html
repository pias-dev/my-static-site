Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. All calculations happen locally in your browser. Your dates are never sent to our servers.

Which date formats can I use?

We use your device’s native date picker, supporting common formats like MM/DD/YYYY (USA) and DD/MM/YYYY (UK/India).

Do you handle leap years and month lengths?

Yes. The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths for accurate results.

How are days counted exclusive or inclusive?

We count calendar days between the start and end date (exclusive of the start date). If you need inclusive results, add one day to the total.

What time zone do you use?

Calculations use UTC to avoid Daylight Saving Time issues and keep results consistent globally.

Can I add or subtract weeks, months, or years?

Yes. Choose Add or Subtract, enter an amount, and pick days, weeks, months, or years to get the new date instantly.

Does adding months handle end of month dates?

We use standard calendar roll over. For dates like Jan 31, results follow real calendar rules. If you need “end of month” anchoring, adjust accordingly.

Is it really free and unlimited?

Yes—lifetime free, fast, and unlimited. No signups, no paywalls, no watermarks.
