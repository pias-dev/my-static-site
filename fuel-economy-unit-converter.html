Formulas and constants

  • 1 mpg (US) = 0.4251437075 km/L
  • 1 mpg (UK/Imp) = 0.354006 km/L
  • L/100 km = 100 ÷ (km/L)
  • MPG (US) = 235.214583 ÷ L/100 km
  • MPG (UK) = 282.480936 ÷ L/100 km
  • MPG (UK) ≈ MPG (US) × 1.20095

Best practices

  • Pick the correct gallon type: US vs UK/Imperial.
  • When comparing international specs, convert to a single unit first.
  • Round only at the end if you’ll do further calculations.
  • Keep original values for auditing and records.
