How to crop an image online

  1. Drag & drop an image or click “Select an Image”.
  2. Choose “Start Freeform Crop” or select an Aspect Ratio preset.
  3. Drag to move/resize the crop box; use handles for proportional resize.
  4. Click “Apply Crop”, choose PNG/JPG/WEBP, then “Download Image”.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Which formats can I use?

Upload JPG, PNG, and WebP. Export to PNG, JPG, or WebP from the format dropdown.

Do you support common presets?

Yes 1:1, 4:5, 16:9, 9:16, 2:3, passport/ID, and popular print sizes (4×6, 5×7).

Is it really free forever?

It’s lifetime free and unlimited no signup, no watermarks, no hidden limits.

Does cropping affect quality?

We preserve resolution at export. Choose PNG/WebP for crisp quality or JPG with high quality for smaller files.

Any tips for passport photos?

Use the closest aspect ratio preset and center the face. Always confirm exact pixel dimensions with your country’s official guidelines.
