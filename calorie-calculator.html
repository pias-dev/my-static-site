How we calculate your calories (BMR → TDEE)

  • BMR: Revised Harris–Benedict equation based on gender, age, weight, and height.
  • TDEE: BMR × your activity level (1.2–1.9) to estimate daily energy needs.
  • Goal calories: Apply a mild (±250 kcal) or standard (±500 kcal) change for loss or gain.
  • Macros: Convert target calories to grams using your selected split.

How to use this calorie calculator

  1. Select your gender and units (Metric or Imperial).
  2. Enter age, weight, and height.
  3. Choose your weekly activity level.
  4. Pick your goal (maintain, loss, gain) and macro split.
  5. Click “Calculate” to see TDEE, goal calories, and daily macros.
