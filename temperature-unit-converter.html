Best practices

  • Use the correct symbols: °C and °F include the degree symbol; Kelvin is written as K (no degree symbol).
  • Kelvin cannot be negative 0 K is absolute zero.
  • Round only at the end if you’ll do further calculations.
  • Keep the original values for auditing and records.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert °C to °F?

Use F = (C × 9/5) + 32. Example: 25 °C = 77 °F.

How do I convert °F to °C?

Use C = (F − 32) × 5/9. Example: 68 °F = 20 °C.

How do I convert °C to K?

Use K = C + 273.15. Example: 0 °C = 273.15 K.
