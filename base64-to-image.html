How to convert Base64 to an image

  1. Paste a full data URI (e.g., data:image/png;base64,AAA...) or drop a .txt file.
  2. Click “Convert” or wait for the auto preview.
  3. Enter a filename and choose PNG/JPG/WEBP.
  4. Click “Download Image”.

Tip: If you only have raw Base64, add the correct prefix (e.g., data:image/jpeg;base64,) before converting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

Which formats can I use?

PNG, JPG/JPEG, WebP for preview and download. SVG data URIs preview in most browsers.

Do I need the data URI prefix?

Yes. Use data:image/<type>;base64, followed by your Base64 string.

Is it really free forever?

It’s lifetime free and unlimited no signup, no watermarks, no hidden limits.

Why won’t my image show?

Check for a valid prefix, correct format match (PNG/JPG/WebP), and non corrupted Base64.

Can I use it offline?

If cached, many browsers will still run it locally. For guaranteed offline use, save the page or use a PWA setup.
