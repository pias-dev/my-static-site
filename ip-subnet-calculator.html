How to use this tool

  1. Enter an IPv4 address (e.g., 192.168.1.100).
  2. Enter a subnet in CIDR (e.g., /24) or mask (e.g., 255.255.255.0) the tool converts both ways.
  3. Click “Calculate” to get network address, broadcast, usable host range, masks, and binary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. All calculations happen in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

Which inputs are supported?

Enter IPv4 and CIDR (e.g., 10.0.0.1/24) or IPv4 with a mask (e.g., 10.0.0.1 and 255.255.255.0).

What is a wildcard mask?

It’s the inverse of the subnet mask. Commonly used for firewall/ACL match rules (e.g., 0.0.0.255 for /24).
