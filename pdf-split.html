How to split PDF files

  1. Upload your PDF (drag & drop or select a file).
  2. Choose “Split by Ranges” or “Split by Interval.”
  3. Enter ranges like 1-3,5,8-10 or a number like 2 (every 2 pages).
  4. Click “Process PDF” and download your files.
  5. Everything runs locally your data stays private.

Tip: For very large PDFs, split in chunks for faster processing.

Best practices

  • Double-check your ranges before processing.
  • Remove passwords from PDFs before splitting.
  • Use clear filenames to stay organized post-split.
  • Verify each output file to confirm page order and quality.
  • Keep originals in case you need to re-split later.
