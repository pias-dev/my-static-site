Best practices

  • Know the difference between bits (b) and bytes (B).
  • Confirm whether you need decimal (KB, MB, GB) or binary (KiB, MiB, GiB) units.
  • Manufacturers often use decimal; OS tools often show binary expect display differences.
  • Round only at the end if you’ll do further calculations.
  • Keep original values for auditing and records.
