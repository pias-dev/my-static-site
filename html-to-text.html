How to convert HTML to plain text

  1. Paste or type your HTML into the HTML Input box.
  2. Click Convert to Text to strip HTML tags and extract clean, readable text.
  3. Review the result in the Plain Text Output area and make edits if needed.
  4. Use Copy or Download to save your text.

Tip: Everything runs locally in your browser (100% private). Press Ctrl/⌘ + Enter to convert instantly.
