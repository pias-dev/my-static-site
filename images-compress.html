How to compress images online

  1. Drag & drop images or click “Select Images”.
  2. Choose Manual Quality or Target Size (KB/MB).
  3. Adjust the quality slider or enter your target file size.
  4. Click “Compress”, then download files individually or as a ZIP.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Which formats can I use?

Upload JPG, PNG, or WebP. For best compression, results export as JPG.

Can I hit an exact size (e.g., 100 KB)?

Yes. Use Target Size, enter 100 (KB), and compress. The tool aims to get as close as possible.

Is it really free forever?

Yes free and unlimited. No signup, no watermarks, no hidden limits.

What about transparency and EXIF?

JPG does not support transparency, so transparent PNG/WebP will have a solid background. EXIF metadata is removed for privacy.

Yes, add multiple images and download all results as a ZIP.
