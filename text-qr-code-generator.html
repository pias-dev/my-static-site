How to create a QR code

  1. Enter your text (URL, message, email, phone, etc.).
  2. Customize colors and optionally upload a logo.
  3. Click “Generate” to preview.
  4. Download PNG or PDF (high-resolution).
  5. Test by scanning with different devices/apps.

Tip: Use strong contrast (dark foreground on light background) and avoid busy backgrounds for best scan results.

Best practices

  • Use high contrast; black on white is ideal.
  • Keep logos modest (10–25% of QR width) to preserve scanability.
  • Print at sufficient size for viewing distance; bigger is safer.
  • Test across devices and QR scanning apps before publishing.
  • For print, prefer PNG or PDF with high resolution and margins.
