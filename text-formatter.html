How to format text online

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box.
  2. Open a dropdown (Case Converter, Cleaner, Duplicates, Sorter) and choose the operation you need.
  3. Chain multiple actions as needed for example: Trim Line Whitespace → Remove Duplicate Lines → Sort Lines (A–Z).
  4. Click Copy to copy the result, or Download to save a .txt file. Use Undo to revert the last change, or Reset to clear everything.

Core operations (space cleanup, line tools, sorting) work for most languages. Case conversion works best with English and many Latin‑based languages.
