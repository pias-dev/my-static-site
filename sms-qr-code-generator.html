How to create an SMS QR code

  1. Enter a phone number (ideally in international E.164 format, e.g., +15551234567).
  2. Optional: add a message (e.g., “Support request” or a keyword).
  3. Customize colors and optionally upload a logo.
  4. Click “Generate” to preview, then download PNG or PDF.
  5. Test on iPhone and Android camera apps before printing or sharing.

Tip: Keep strong contrast (dark foreground on light background) for best scan results.

Best practices

  • Use international format like +15551234567 for maximum compatibility.
  • Keep logos modest (10–25% of QR width) and avoid busy backgrounds.
  • Test the QR on multiple devices and camera apps.
  • Prefer dark foreground on a light background for reliable scanning.
  • Place printed QRs where lighting and distance make scanning easy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. We don’t upload or store your data.

What is smsto:?

It’s the standard URI format that opens the SMS app with a prefilled number and message.

Which phone formats are best?

Use E.164 (e.g., +15551234567) for consistent results on iOS and Android.

Can I add a custom message?

Yes, the message field is optional. Keep it short and clear to fit any device limits.

Does it work offline?

Yes, once the page loads. Everything is client side fast and private.

My QR doesn’t scan help?
