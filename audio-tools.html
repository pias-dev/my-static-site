Will converting MP3 to WAV improve quality?

No. WAV is lossless, but converting a lossy MP3 to WAV won’t recover lost detail. Use WAV for editing/mastering or when a DAW requires it.

What MP3 bitrate should I choose?

128 kbps: smaller size for voice. 192 kbps: good balance for music. 256–320 kbps: higher quality for music and mastering.
