How to resize PDF pages

  1. Upload your PDF (drag & drop or select file).
  2. Choose a standard size (A4, A5, US Letter, Legal) or set custom width/height.
  3. Pick units (mm or inches) and decide whether to maintain aspect ratio.
  4. Optionally specify page ranges (e.g., 1, 3-5, 8).
  5. Click “Apply & Resize” and download your file.

Note: This tool scales page content to fit the new page size. Vector content remains sharp; images keep their original resolution.

Best practices

  • For mixed orientations, apply resizing to specific pages as needed.
  • If a PDF is password protected, remove protection first.
  • When targeting exact print specs, use mm units and standard sizes.
  • Re export corrupted PDFs from the source app and try again.
  • Keep the original as a backup for re resizing later.
