How to calculate fertilizer rates

  1. Select your crop preset (or “Custom”), soil type, and field size (hectares or acres).
  2. Enter target N, P, and K (elemental) the tool converts P to P₂O₅ and K to K₂O automatically.
  3. Optional: enable Split Application to top-dress part of the N (often as Urea).
  4. Enter local prices for Urea, DAP, MOP, or define a custom blend and strategy (P‑based or K‑based).
  5. Click “Calculate” to get rates, total amounts, nutrient balance, and costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it private and secure?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

Elemental P vs P₂O₅? K vs K₂O?

Targets are elemental P and K. The tool converts to P₂O₅ (×2.291) and K₂O (×1.205) for fertilizer sources.

Which fertilizers are included?

Urea (46‑0‑0), DAP (18‑46‑0), MOP (0‑0‑60), and custom blends with N‑P₂O₅‑K₂O percentages.

Can I do split applications?

Yes, typically for N. P and K are often applied at planting; adjust as local guidance suggests.

Is it really free?

Yes lifetime free and unlimited. No signups, no paywalls, no watermarks.

Does this replace soil tests?

No. Always use recent soil tests and local recommendations for final decisions.
