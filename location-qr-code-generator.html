How to create a location QR code

  1. Select “Coordinates” or “Address”.
  2. Enter latitude and longitude, or a full address.
  3. Optional: add a label, choose colors, and upload a logo.
  4. Click “Generate” to preview, then download PNG or PDF.
  5. Test by scanning with your phone so it opens in Maps.

Tip: High contrast (dark code on light background) and modest logo size improve scan reliability.

Best practices

  • Use dark foreground on a light background for best scanning.
  • Keep logos 10–25% of the QR width and leave quiet zone margins.
  • Use precise coordinates for outdoor locations; use addresses for venues.
  • Test on Android and iOS; print at least 2.5–3 cm (1–1.2 in) for short scan distance.
  • Place codes where phones can focus (avoid glossy, curved, or moving surfaces).
