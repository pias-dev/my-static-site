    How to merge PDF files

    1. Upload your PDFs (drag & drop or select files).
    2. Arrange file order using the up/down arrows.
    3. Enter your output filename (e.g., merged.pdf).
    4. Click “Merge PDFs” and download your file.
    5. Everything runs locally your data stays private.

    Tip: For very large PDFs, merge in batches for faster processing.

    Best practices

    • Double-check order before merging to avoid rework.
    • Ensure files are not password-protected; remove passwords first.
    • Use clear filenames to stay organized post-merge.
    • Test the final merged PDF to confirm page order and quality.
    • Keep originals in case you need to re-merge later.
