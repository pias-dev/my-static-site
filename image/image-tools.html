A list of frequently asked questions (FAQ)

How is Chronological Age Calculated?

This chronological age calculator measures the exact period from your birth date up to today’s date. It subtracts your birth year, month, and day from the present time of year, month, and day making proper adjustments for leap years to give you an answer in years, months, and days.

Why do I need an “how old am I” calculator?

Why do I need an “how old am I” calculator? It's by far the quickest and most precise way of finding an answer! Be it filling out an official form, planning for a milestone birthday, or out of sheer curiosity, the tool provides, at once and math-free, an extremely detailed breakdown on age.

Is my date of birth information saved?

Absolutely not. Your privacy is our number one concern. The entire calculation happens securely in your web browser. Your DOB is never sent to our servers, ensuring your data remains 100% private and confidential worldwide.

Do you support different date formats?

Yep! We made our online age calculator intuitive and globally friendly. The date picker will use the format standard for your browser and your region. Therefore, you can enter your date of birth in whichever way is most natural to you. It’s no worry, it’ll calculate correctly all the same.
